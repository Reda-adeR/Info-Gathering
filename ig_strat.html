Each card below includes a copy‑ready example caption you can edit. Replace emoji, swap visuals, localize language.

Type 1

Educational Carousel

Purpose: Teach 1 topic in simple steps people can swipe through and save.

Use for: Weather terms, soil basics, “How NDVI works,” fertiliser math.

Tone: Clear, visual, snackable. End with “Save this!”

Slides:

  • Slide 1: Hook question (“What is NDVI?”)
  • Slides 2‑5: Plain points or icons
  • Last slide: CTA + hashtag strip

Example Educational Carousel:

🌱 What is NDVI? (Swipe →)

  • Plants reflect near‑infrared light when healthy.
  • NDVI compares red vs NIR to score “greenness.”
  • High NDVI = active growth; low = stress or bare soil.
  • We map NDVI to show crop hot‑spots in minutes.

Save & share with your agronomy team. #AgriAI

Type 2

Quick Tips Reel

Purpose: Deliver ultra‑short video tips viewers can watch with or without sound.

Use for: “3 irrigation fails,” “How to read soil moisture,” “Spray day checklist.”

Format: 5‑8s clips, bold text overlays, auto‑captions.

Example Quick Tips Reel:

💧 3 Fast Irrigation Checks

  • Check soil 5 cm & 20 cm deep.
  • Skip watering after >20 mm rain.
  • Watch leaf droop mid‑day = stress.

Save for the next hot week! #Irrigation #AgriAI

Type 3

Before / After Visual

Purpose: Show transformation at a glance—tool impact, crop recovery, better data.

Use for: NDVI change, fert response, app vs notebook records.

Format: Split image, slider, or first/second slide carousel.

Example Before/After Post:

📸 Before vs After Precision Fertilizer

Left: uneven growth. Right: 4 weeks after zone‑based application guided by NDVI.

See the difference? Tag someone who needs this.

Type 4

Behind‑the‑Scenes Field / Build

Purpose: Humanize the brand; show real work in fields or code.

Use for: Installing sensors, coding sprints, field data days.

Tone: Casual, real, inclusive (“Come with us to the field”).

Example Behind‑the‑Scenes Post:

🚜 Field day in Nakuru!

Mapping demo fields + stress‑testing our soil snapshot button (bad network, hot sun 😅).

What field condition kills your data collection most? Comment below.

Type 5

Founder / Team Introduction

Purpose: Build trust; put faces to the brand.

Use for: First‑time followers, hiring, community building.

Tone: Warm, personal, short story hook.

Example Founder Intro:

👋 Meet Amina – Co‑founder at Agri AI

Grew up flood‑irrigating by guesswork. Now building tools that tell farmers when to water…or not.

Where did you learn farming? Drop your location 👇

Type 6

Problem → Solution (Pain Point)

Purpose: Show you understand pain; position your product as the fix.

Hook: Shock stat, loss story, urgent emoji.

Example Problem→Solution Post:

😵 40% crop loss from a pest he didn’t catch in time.

No scouting data. No alert. Too late.

Agri AI lets you snap a leaf & get pest ID help fast—before damage spreads.

Seen this? Tell us in comments.

Type 7

Data Bite Graphic

Purpose: Share a punchy number or comparison that sparks saves & shares.

Use for: Fertiliser kg/ha country compare, yield gap, rainfall anomaly.

Example Data Bite:

📊 Fertiliser Use: Kenya vs Nigeria vs Ethiopia

Farm nutrients aren’t evenly applied—see why yields differ.

Full breakdown in bio. #SoilHealth

Type 8

User Quote / Testimonial

Purpose: Social proof—real users, real results.

Visual: Farmer photo, quote card, or audio waveform clip.

Example Testimonial:

🌾 “Your alert saved me a pump run—diesel is too high!”

Musa – Kano, Nigeria. Used Agri AI soil moisture warnings.

Want in? Apply for pilot (link in bio).

Type 9

Partner Spotlight / Collaboration

Purpose: Show ecosystem strength; tag collaborators.

Use for: NGO pilots, co‑ops, research stations.

Example Partner Spotlight:

🤝 Partnering with GreenFields Co‑op

Mapping 200 farms & piloting irrigation alerts this season.

Co‑ops interested in Round 2? DM us.

Type 10

Launch Countdown (Stories / Feed)

Purpose: Build hype before feature drop or beta opening.

Use: 3‑2‑1 countdown slides, reminder sticker.

Example Launch Countdown:

⏳ 3 Days Until Soil Snapshot Beta

Draw your field → instant soil & weather data.

Turn on reminders! #AgriAI #BetaLaunch

Type 11

Story Poll / Quiz

Purpose: Drive taps & gather quick audience data.

Use: Poll: “What stresses your crop most?” Quiz: “Which soil holds most water?”

Example Story Poll:

📊 What hurts your yields more?

  • Not enough rain
  • Unknown pests
  • Fertilizer cost
  • Labor shortage

Vote in our Story!

Type 12

Mini Tutorial Carousel

Purpose: Show exactly how to do 1 action in your app.

Use: “Upload your field,” “Check soil health,” “Share farm team access.”

Example Tutorial:

📲 How to Add Your Field in Agri AI (Swipe)

  1. Tap + Add Field
  2. Draw boundary or upload file
  3. Save & get soil data

Need help? DM us.

Type 13

Myth vs Fact Slide

Purpose: Correct common misunderstandings in farming.

Use: Rain vs irrigation, fertiliser myths, pest ID myths.

Example Myth vs Fact:

❌ Myth: “If it rained, I don’t need to irrigate.”

✅ Fact: Sandy soils drain fast—check soil moisture before skipping water.

More tips in bio. #SoilMoisture

Type 14

Relatable Meme / Humor

Purpose: Reach & relatability; human side of ag work.

Use: Weather fails, soil jokes, spreadsheet pain.

Example Meme Caption:

😂 When the forecast says “clear” and you harvest… then it pours.

Tag a friend who trusts the sky more than data.

Type 15

Giveaway / Contest

Purpose: Grow followers & gather user stories.

Use: Tag‑to‑win, submit field photo, share best yield tip.

Example Giveaway:

🎁 Win a Soil Test Kit!

  1. Follow @AgriAI
  2. Tag 2 farmers
  3. Comment your crop

Winner Fri. Not affiliated with Instagram.

Type 16

Live Session Promo

Purpose: Drive real‑time engagement & Q&A.

Use: Feature launch demo, agronomy AMA, “ask us about NDVI.”

Example Live Promo:

Join us 18:00 EAT—bring your irrigation questions.

Add reminder in Stories! #AgriAI #IGLive

Suggested Posting Strategy for Pre-Launch:

  1. Month 1-2: Focus on educational content and teasers (70% value, 30% hype).
  2. Month 3: Ramp up team stories and problem-solution posts.
  3. Launch Week: Countdowns, announcements, and live demos.
  4. Post-Launch: Testimonials, milestones, and engagement posts.

Pro Tips:

  • Mix formats: Text, carousels, videos, and polls.
  • Post consistently: 2-3x/week (quality > quantity).
  • Engage daily: Comment on industry posts to increase visibility.
  • Use hashtags: #Startups #YourIndustry #Innovation.
